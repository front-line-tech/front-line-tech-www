FULL privacy policy

This is the FULL privacy policy for Front-Line Tech Ltd apps.

This policy applies to the following apps:

  • Bothie

Apps compliant with this policy uphold the following privacy principles:

  • The app may only collect data in order to offer the functionality described by the app.
  • The app will not transmit any data back to Front-Line Tech Ltd (this includes anonymised analytics data) without your expressed permission.
  • The app will not transmit any data to any third party without first making clear to you exactly what it is doing, and why, and requesting your permission to do so. Without this permission, the app will not expose or transmit your data.
  • Some anonymised installation and crash data is made available to Front-Line Tech Ltd through the Google Play Store, and the Apple App Store. This will only be used to enhance the service provided by the app.
  • Wherever practically possible, the app will allow you to delete any data stored by the app on your device, at any time.
  • If that is not practically possible, the data stored by the app on your device will be deletable through standard tools on your device.

    ie. photos captured by the app at your request will be stored in your device's photo gallery, and may be deleted through the tools native to that device.

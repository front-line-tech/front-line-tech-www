Commissioner's 100 site

The Commissioner's 100 is a movement of front line officers and staff from the Metropolitan Police who want to harness the energy and spirit of frontline officers for good.

They've been working to improve the Met and make Total Policing happen from the bottom up rather than just the top down.

Front-Line Tech Ltd maintains and develops the Commissioner's 100 site on a voluntary basis, allowing the group to express themselves publically and allowing them to highlight the work they are doing in the Met and in the greater policing world - to bring about change and improvement to policing practises.

Hackathon

We are dedicated to locating and supporting the great talent and passion that exists in the independent software development and hackathon community.

In the summer of 2013, founders of Front Line Tech Ltd set up and ran the first UK Policing Hackathon - Hack the Police, as part of a volunteer effort to show just how much independent developers can achieve.

The hackathon was a great success, and Front Line Tech looks forward to supporting future efforts, hackathons, and challenges to drive forward technologies that can enhance policing, keep the public safe, and create savings across public service.

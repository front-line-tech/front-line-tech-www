Hackathon

We are dedicated to locating and supporting the great talent and passion that exists in the independent software development and hackathon community.

Founders of Front Line Tech Ltd set up and ran the first UK Policing Hackathon - Hack the Police, as part of a volunteer effort to show just how much independent developers can achieve.

The hackathon was a great success, and Front Line Tech looks forward to supporting future efforts, hackathons, and challenges to drive forward technologies that can enhance policing, keep the public safe, and create savings across public service.

What is Aisle3?

Aisle3 is a C# Android library built to provide your mobile app with features that allow it to store sensitive data on your mobile device. It does so in an accreditable and secure fashion, allowing you to quickly and easily build secure connections to remote services - and easily document the steps you have taken to protect your assets.

Aisle3 incorporates best practises surrounding information lifetimes, encryption and remote wipe.

Untrusted devices

There are many vectors an attacker might use to recover your sensitive data. Some involve the use of a compromised version of Android, or a malicious app with enough authority to see your application data. Aisle3 is frequently updated to protect against known threats like these.

Secure storage

As Aisle3 is designed to run on a wide range of modern Android devices, it cannot rely on the OS to provide secure encryption or sandboxing. Instead, it creates its own encrypted data store inside the confines of the OS sandbox - and allows you to persist your data and objects there. Even if the application's sandbox is compromised, your data cannot be decrypted without the appropriate keys.

Communicate securely

Aisle3 simplifies the process of setting up secure TLS connections, the transfer of data, the storage of that data in encrypted form, and mitigating risks such as man-in-the-middle attacks.

Pin certificates

Aisle3 supports certificate pinning - a way to ensure that the service you are communicating with is your own. To do this, Aisle3 observes the signatures and certificates provided by the remote server, and alerts your app when it detects an attack.

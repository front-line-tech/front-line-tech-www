Aisle3

A secure mobile communications and storage and communications library to protect your sensitive data.

What is Aisle3?

Aisle3 is an Android library that provides your mobile app with features that allow it to store sensitive data on your mobile device in an accreditable and secure fashion, and allows you to quickly and easily build secure connections to remote services.

Aisle3 incorporates best practises surrounding information lifetimes, encryption and remote wipe.

Beware the device

There are many attack vectors for an attacker to use to get hold of your sensitive data. Some involve the use of a compromised version of Android, or a malicious app running on Android with enough authority to see your application data.

Secure storage

Aisle3 does not rely on the OS to provide secure encryption or sandboxing. Instead, it creates its own encrypted data store inside the confines of the OS sandbox - and allows you to persist your data and objects there. Even if the app's sandbox is compromised, your data cannot be decrypted without the appropriate keys.

Communicate securely

Aisle3 simplifies the process of setting up secure TLS connections, the transfer of data, the storage of that data in encrypted form, and mitigating risks such as man-in-the-middle attacks.

Pin certificates

Aisle3 supports certificate pinning - a way to ensure that the remote service you are communicating with is your own service and that there is not a 3rd-party sitting between you and the service compromising the your secrecy. To do this, Aisle3 observes the signatures and certificates provided by the remote server, and alerts the app when it detects an attack.
